Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send prescriptions to Capsule?

You can send prescriptions to Capsule through your e-prescribing system. Search for "Capsule Pharmacy" and our information will appear.

Does Capsule handle controlled substances?

Yes, Capsule can handle most controlled substance prescriptions, including Schedule II-V medications, in accordance with state and federal regulations.

How does Capsule handle prior authorizations?

Capsule manages the prior authorization process digitally, minimizing paperwork for your practice. We'll reach out to your office electronically when authorization is needed.

Where does Capsule deliver?

Capsule delivers to patients in many major metropolitan areas. Check our coverage page to see if we deliver in your area.

Is there any cost to partner with Capsule?

No, there's no cost for healthcare providers to partner with Capsule. We work with insurance just like a traditional pharmacy.
