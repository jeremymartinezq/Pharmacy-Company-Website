Our Story

From Idea to Innovation

Founded in 2020, Capsule Pharmacy began with a simple mission: to make prescription medications more accessible and affordable for everyone. What started as a small team of healthcare professionals and tech enthusiasts has grown into a nationwide network of pharmacies and healthcare providers.

Our founders, Emily Turner and Michael Rodriguez, experienced firsthand the challenges patients face when trying to access affordable medications. They combined their expertise in healthcare and technology to create a solution that would bridge the gap between patients, doctors, and pharmacies.
