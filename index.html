Frequently Asked Questions

How do I transfer my prescriptions to Union Pharmacy?

It's simple! Just sign up for Union Pharmacy and we'll handle transferring your prescriptions from your old pharmacy. You can also ask your doctor to send new prescriptions directly to Union Pharmacy.

Is there a delivery fee?

No, delivery is completely free. We deliver the same day, with no minimum order requirements.

Does Union Pharmacy accept my insurance?

Yes, Union Pharmacy accepts all major insurance plans. We'll work with your insurance to get you the best price for your medications.

Where does Union Pharmacy deliver?

Union Pharmacy currently delivers to many major metropolitan areas. Enter your zip code at the top of the page to see if we deliver to your area.
